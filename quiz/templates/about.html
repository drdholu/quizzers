About Quiz App

Quizzers is a web application that allows users to take quizzes on various topics, track their progress, and improve their knowledge. The app provides a user-friendly interface and a wide range of quizzes to choose from.

Our mission is to make learning fun and engaging for everyone. Whether you're a student, a professional, or just someone who loves to learn, Quizzers has something for you.

Features

  • Variety of quizzes on different topics
  • User-friendly interface
  • Score Tracking
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Secure authentication and authorization
